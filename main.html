UPES ACM STUDENT CHAPTER

UPES-ACM Student Chapter started its activity from April 2010 by organizing a Poster Presentation competition on the theme ‘IT Applications in Energy Sectors’. We also organized Paper Presentation competition on “Green Computing” besides community program in the year 2010 and 2011 for Computer Awareness. We participated in this program and taught computer skills to students from nearby village schools organized by Corporate Social Responsibility cell of the University.
